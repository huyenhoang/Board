Blitz Chess

Chess for the 21st century

Welcome to Blitz, a modern way to play chess that promotes customization, faster game play, dynamic strategy and metagames.

Blitz is a different from traditional chess sites. Blitz has some unique features like custom playing boards, discussion forms, and customized playing pieces through unlockable themes. But most importantly, Blitz features a revolutionary way to play chess never seen before - custom starting position for the Knights, Bishop, and Rook. Gone are the days of developing an excitment for chess at an early age only to lose it later on when one discover that at the highest level, the game is all about who can memorize all the possibility moves rather than strategy.
